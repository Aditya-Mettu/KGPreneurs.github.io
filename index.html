ABOUT

KGPrenuer provides an opportunity for any KGPian working on his own idea and has his own startup or anyone who ideated or piloted something, and is willing to make that idea take shape and come to life
Just show us any work you are doing or have done in the past that has the potential for a startup - a business model presentation, a pilot run, or anything that is monetizable and impactful, and we will remove your past roadblock by providing you with support for the same.

We wish to unearth new talents from within the campus, and boost/headstart the careers of those who participated in the program..
